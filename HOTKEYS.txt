HOTKEYS in normal mode
Tabs:
	Alt-Tab - next tab
	Alt-. - next tab too
	Alt-, - previous tab
	SPC W - close tab

Comments(only custom):
	SPC / - toggle line comment

NeoTree:
	SPC e - focus NeoTree

Terminal:
	SPC h - Open horizontal terminal
	SPC v - Open vertical terminal
	SPC f - Open floating terminal

Telescope:
	t f - find files
	t g - live grep

Web Search:
	SPC s - search

Other:
	"-" - open mini.files
	SPC q q a a - force quit DuploVim (USE CAREFULLY!!!)
	SPC h - display this help
